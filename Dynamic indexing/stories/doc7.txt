Once upon a time there was a lion called Leo and a hunter called Josh.

One day while the lion was eating, Josh spotted him. Leo didn't see the hunter but he heard him and ran away.

Josh was chasing him through the jungle but Leo was to fast. He found a cave to hide in and Josh got lost.

Leo the lion found Josh and helped him find his way out of the jungle.

Josh didn't hunt Leo anymore because he was a nice lion and helped Josh.
